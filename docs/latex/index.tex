\hypertarget{index_autotoc_md2}{}\doxysubsubsection{Project Overview and Description}\label{index_autotoc_md2}
Since robots are being rapidly deployed for various domestic applications, Acme Robotics is looking to develop a domestic robot as a part of its 5-\/year product road-\/map. The robot called the \char`\"{}\+Domestic Decluttering Robot\char`\"{} will declutter the house floor by identifying different items, collecting them, and placing them at their desired location in the house. The robot will scout the house floor to detect and grasp the desired object. Once grasped, it will place the object at the predefined location.
